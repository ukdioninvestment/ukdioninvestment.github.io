FAQ: Frequently Asked Question

Ans:

7. What happens if I decide to pull out my investment before the end of the investment tenure?


Ans:

    At UK-DION Investment Ltd, our top priority is our client. If an investor wants to pull out of his plan, a portfolio manager is assigned to find out the reasons behind the investor's decision and try to resolve it without collapsing the investment. This can be done by issuing a minimal interest loan to the investor.

    UK-DION Investment will invoke the undertaking and contractual obligations in section D between UK-DION and the investor in Quote " I am duty bound not to make any withdrawal either whole or part of my investment capital within the stipulated period of my investment. I accept that the company will not entertain any pre-liquidation request if my investment is less than 6months.
    If however I wish to pre-liquidate after 6 months but before maturity of the agreed tenure, I accept to forfeit all accrued interest from inception of the investment ".
