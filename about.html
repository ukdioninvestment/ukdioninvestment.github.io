About Us

UK-DION INVESTMENT LTD is an investment company registered in Nigeria with Corporate Affairs Commission and insured by Cornerstone Insurance Plc. We are concerned individuals determined to help people become financially independent, creating wealth through massive investments here in Nigeria. We believe in hard work and working together to achieve the financial growth we desperately desire in Nigeria. We believe we can increase the standard of living for Nigerians by encouraging investment whilst creating jobs to keep the youths engaged. We are a key player in the wealth management, child trust, real estate, securities and money market instruments.
