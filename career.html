WORKING WITH US

You will be joining a fast paced, intellectually tasking environment where innovation is part of the everyday; where knowledge is readily shared; and where everyone is committed to making us the “MOST SOUGHT AFTER INVESTMENT COMPANY” for our customers and clients. We award performance and provide the training and development, leadership, mentoring and support necessary for staff growth.

UK-DION Entry-Level

Are you driven, confident and enthusiastic, would love to join our team of professionals in creating wealth, jobs and entrepreneurs? Would you like to make your passion your career? Are you willing to join us in our journey to becoming the most sought after Investment Company in Nigeria? Then UK-DION Entry Level is just for YOU!
Your career journey with us starts with a 4-month probationary period, which gives you an opportunity to improve your marketing and financial management skills.

Who we are looking for

Marketing Executives:
Requirements:
• Must be knowledgeable in investment banking.
• Marketers with current existing client base will be considered first.
• Must be able to work in a competitive and target based environment.
• Must posses at least two years experience in investment marketing.
• Experience in the banking/financial service sector is an added advantage.

Client Service Representatives:

Requirements:
• Highly driven individuals, goal oriented with persuasive skills and ability to work in a target based environment.
• He/she should be a fast learner, possess effective communication skills, interpersonal skills and time management skills.
