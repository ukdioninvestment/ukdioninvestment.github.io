About Nnenna

HEAD OF COMPLIANCE

Nnenna Ikubogh is a Nigerian citizen and a seasoned lawyer by profession. She acquired an LLB (Law) in Imo State University where she graduated as the second best graduating student in Law faculty. She further acquired a BL (Law) in Nigerian Law School, Abuja, and is currently at the verge of completing masters dregree in Law (LLm) in Rivers State University Port Harcourt.

She has worked as state counsel in Nasarawa State Judiciary, Lafia, and as Legal practitioner in reputable law firms in Nigeria before taking up the role of Personal Financial Consultant in a prestigious commercial bank in Nigeria where she also worked as Compliance Officer and Relationship Manager. She has over 9 years active working experience in the banking industry with outstanding achievements at the various offices held.



Nnenna Ikubogh is a capable and knowledgeable compliance manager who is well versed in legal guidelines and corporate governance best practices. She is able to promote, develop, and establish a positive safety culture within a company so that it can meet its legal requirements in terms of compliance with governing bodies. Nnenna has a track record of helping companies to manage risk, maintain a positive reputation and avoid law suits. She is an accomplished analytical thinker and passionate problem solver who has the ability to read and interpret complex regulations and make appropriate operational decisions regarding them.

On a personal level, Nnenna is proactive , motivated and has a high level of customer service orientation.
