Smart Savers Account

The UK-DION SMART SAVERS ACCOUNT is an initiative of Uk-Dion Investment Limited to help low and medium income/profit earners to save a minimum of N50,000 monthly and also to partake in our competitive interest rates.

Features
• Minimum investment of ₦ 50,000.00
• Minimum Savings Tenure is 12months
• Interest rate of 6% ₦50,000.00 - ₦99,000, 8%; ₦100,000 - ₦249,000; 10% ₦250 and above.
• Option to convert minimum savings of N1million to Individual Investment package.
• Total savings and interest accrued is paid on the 13th month.

Interest Rate
• Cumulative interest rate of 6% on ₦50,000 - ₦99,000.
• Cumulative interest rate of 8% on ₦100,000 - ₦249,000.
• Cumulative interest rate of 10% on ₦250,000 and above.
• Convert to Individual investment plan at 17.5% interest rate from N1,000,000.

Tenure
• Twelve (12) months.
• Return on investment on the 13th month.
• Option to roll-over funds at the end of tenure or convert to individual investment plan

Days Of Payment
• All payments must be made on or before the 4th of every month.
• A 3 days period of grace for payment to the 7th of every month .

Default
• Late payment or no payment for any month attracts a 25% deduction on the cumulative interests on savings.

Withdrawals
• No withdrawals before 6months of investments or forfeit 20% on capital investment.
• Withdrawals after 6 months of investments before maturity date incurs the loss of all interest accruable on savings .

Notifications
• Monthly Notifications Of Receipt Of Payment Via Email Or Text Message.
