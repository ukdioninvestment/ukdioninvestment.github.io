Who we are

UK-DION INVESTEMENT LTD is a registered investment company with the Nigerian Corporate Affairs Commission (RC 1295058) We offer financial advice to our investors and partners, create job opportunities and encourage entrepreneurship.

Born in 2015, we’ve built our firm on a strong foundation of integrity and professional ethics and are bound by law to put our clients’ interest before our own We help our partners/investors to 1. Plan for family trust. 2. Enjoy a monthly income while sitting at home. We are determined to create financial independence in the country through massive investment here in Nigeria, we want our investors to benefit from the opportunities in the Capital Market both domestically and abroad hence investments are done both national and internationally. Uk Dion Investments are made on real estates, transportation, agriculture and natural gas.
