About Us

A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

UK-DION INVESTMENT LTD is an investment company registered in Nigeria with Corporate Affairs Commission. We are concerned individuals determined to help people become financial independent, creating wealth through massive investments here in Nigeria, in doing this, we create job opportunities and encourage entrepreneurship for young people in Nigeria. We believe in hard work and working together to achieve the financial growth we desperately desire in Nigeria. We believe we can increase the standard of living for Nigerians by encouraging investment whilst creating jobs to keep the youths engaged.
