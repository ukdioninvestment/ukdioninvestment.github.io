Welcome to UK-DION INVESTMENT LTD

UK-DION INVESTMENT LTD is an Asset management company which is registered with Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) and insured by Cornerstone Insurance Plc. Our philosophy is to provide our clients with the best independent advice on their investments and help them get the best value for their money.

Who we are

UK-DION INVESTEMENT LTD is a non-banking financial institution which is into the business of asset/wealth management. The company is built on a strong foundation of integrity and professional ethics and bound by law to put clients interest first, thus we assist our clients/ investors to:
1. Plan for family trust,
2. Enjoy a steady stream of income and
3. Plan for future projects.
We create financial independence through massive investment here in Nigeria, and ensure that our clients/investors benefit from the capital market and other lucrative return yielding sectors.

Our Partners

We have partners both abroad and in Nigeria working together with the same goal of helping people invest their money wisely, this in return yields them great profits over the years rather than having their money sit dormant in banks.

We believe:

That for an investment to be successful, it must be built on a set of core principles which is evident in our investment diversification, spreading a portfolio across different asset and not just on stocks and bonds.

That in working hard together we will achieve financial growth which is desperately needed in the country.
